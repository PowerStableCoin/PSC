About DAOPowerStableCoin

DAOPowerStableCoin (PSC) is a decentralized, collateral-backed stablecoin designed to bring stability and innovative user incentives to the DeFi ecosystem. Every PSC token is fully backed by locked collateral, and the ecosystem includes bonus rewards for payments and staking opportunities.

How It Works

Collateral Management

Users mint PSC by locking approved stablecoin collateral. The contract determines the value using multiple oracle price feeds, calculates a median price, and mints PSC accordingly.

Payment & Bonus

Merchants register to accept PSC. When users make payments, they receive bonus tokens (cashback) drawn from a bonus pool, encouraging widespread adoption.

Staking for Rewards

PSC holders can stake their tokens to earn additional yield from a dedicated staking reward pool. This reduces the circulating supply and rewards long-term holders.

Decentralized Governance

Key parameters are governed by a DAO, ensuring transparency and community participation in the protocol's evolution.
