About DAOPowerStableCoin

DAOPowerStableCoin (PSC) is a next-generation stablecoin that combines robust collateralization with dynamic rewards for everyday use. Built on decentralized governance, PSC ensures every token is fully backed while rewarding users through bonus cashback and staking yields.

Join our revolution where finance meets innovation—experience stability, transparency, and growth like never before.

How It Works

Collateral-Backed Minting

Users lock approved stablecoin collateral to mint PSC. The collateral is valued using multiple oracle feeds to ensure fair pricing.

Payment & Bonus Rewards

Merchants accept PSC as payment. Users receive bonus rewards (cashback) on their payments, incentivizing everyday transactions.

Staking for Yield

PSC holders can stake their tokens to earn additional yield. This mechanism reduces circulating supply and rewards long-term commitment.

Decentralized Governance

The protocol’s parameters are governed by a DAO, ensuring that key decisions are transparent and community-driven.
